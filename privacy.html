Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30|9|24

At TrackWeNg, we understand that privacy is important, and we are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our location-sharing app. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To make your experience with our app better and more personalized, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account or interact with the app, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location Data: Our app uses your device’s location to enable location sharing with your friends and family. You can control this through your device’s settings.
  • Device Information: We may also collect information about your device, such as your device type, operating system version, and unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Location Sharing: Allow you to share your real-time location with people you trust, such as friends and family.
  • Improve the App: Make improvements to the app, fix issues, and add new features based on how you use it.
  • Send You Updates: Keep you informed about important changes, features, or support-related messages.

3. Who We Share Your Information With

We value your privacy, and we do not share your data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your location with people you choose to share it with within the app.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, or if necessary to protect the safety, rights, or property of you, us, or others, we may disclose your information.

4. Data Protection and Security

We take security seriously and use reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please keep in mind that no security system is 100% foolproof, and we will always continuously try to guarantee complete protection of your information.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Location Settings: You can turn off location sharing at any time by adjusting your device’s settings. Note that turning off location sharing may limit some features of the app.
  • Account Management: You can update or delete your account information by reaching out to us through the app or do it by yourself.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have unintentionally collected such data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

From time to time, we may update this Privacy Policy. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or other communication channels. Please check this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
